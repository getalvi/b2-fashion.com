Our Story

Fashion That Speaks for You

B2 Fashion is one of Bangladesh's premier fashion destinations, offering a curated selection of export-quality attire for the modern woman and man. Inaugurated in Gulshan, Dhaka in September 2021 by celebrated performer Tahsan Khan, our brand has quickly become a beloved name in Dhaka's fashion scene.

Our CEO Sushrita Poddar Bithi and the entire B2 family are passionate about bringing international quality and style to every customer, with locations across Barisal, Chittagong, and our flagship Gulshan store.
